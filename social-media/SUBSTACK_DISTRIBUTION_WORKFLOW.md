# Substack Distribution Workflow

Reusable workflow for long-form newsletter/article production and distribution.

## Strategy

- Prioritize quality and audience relevance over posting frequency.
- Use social teasers to drive discovery of long-form work.
- Optionally future-date publication to support pre-release subscription intent.

## Phase 1: Planning

1. Define audience problem and article promise.
2. Generate and select hook options.
3. Lock structure:
   - hook
   - beat 1
   - beat 2
   - beat 3
   - twist
   - resolution
4. Confirm confidence in structure before drafting full prose.

## Phase 2: Drafting

1. Draft article body with one argument carried across all beats.
2. Use concrete examples and mechanisms, not abstract commentary.
3. Keep every section tied to reader value.

## Phase 3: Review

1. Check factual defensibility.
2. Remove filler and repeated points.
3. Validate transitions and pacing across beats.
4. Confirm clear takeaway and CTA.

## Phase 4: Distribution

1. Extract 3-5 teaser snippets from completed article.
2. Create platform-native promotion drafts.
3. Schedule/publish long-form asset.
4. Post teaser content with destination link strategy.
5. Track opens, clicks, and conversion performance.

## Formatting Guidance

- Write in markdown first when useful for drafting clarity.
- Convert to platform-compatible rich text before publishing if needed.

## Output Contract

For each article cycle, produce:

- final long-form draft
- teaser snippet set
- cross-platform promotion drafts
- launch checklist and metrics plan
