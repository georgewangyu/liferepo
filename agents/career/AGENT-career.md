# AGENT-career

## Purpose

Define reusable workflows for job search execution and interview prep systems.

## Job-Hunting Pipeline Contract

1. Maintain target-company list and current status.
2. Track each application from draft to outcome.
3. Keep referral and follow-up actions explicit.
4. Review weekly conversion metrics and adjust strategy.

## Interview Prep Contract

- Maintain separate tracks:
  - coding practice
  - system design
  - company-specific prep
- Record weak areas and concrete remediation tasks.
- Reuse structured explanation formats across practice sessions.

## Resume Integration

- Use role-specific resume variants.
- Feed interview and application feedback back into resume bullets.
- Keep a clear mapping from target role requirements to evidence in resume.

## Privacy

Private company notes, referral names, and personal hiring details remain in
`<private-repo>`.
