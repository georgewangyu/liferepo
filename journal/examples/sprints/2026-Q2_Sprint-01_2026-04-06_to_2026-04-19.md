# Sprint 01 — 2026-Q2 (2026-04-06 to 2026-04-19)

## Sprint Theme

- **Re-entry sprint**: stabilize schedule after travel and re-establish
  consistent morning deep work.
- **Success = restore rhythm + ship visible progress in core domains.**

### Context from Previous Retro + Reflections

- Work rhythm degraded during travel-heavy weeks because setup constraints and
  context switching reduced deep-focus consistency.
- Interview and career threads stayed active but execution quality was uneven.
- Content output became reactive instead of planned.

## Sprint Mode

- **Selected mode**: **Standard Mode**
- **Why**:
  - Normal workspace setup is available.
  - Priority is rebuilding stable cadence, not sprinting for stretch output.

## Sprint Pattern

*See `journal/SPRINT_PATTERN.md` and `journal/sprints/README.md` in private
runtime repo for mode rules.*

**Monday–Friday structure:**
- **Morning sprint**: hardest/most important work.
- **Work sprints**: 1–2 execution blocks during work hours.
- **Evening sprint**: optional 1 block for content or project continuity.

## Sprint Targets

- **Total deep sprint target**: `24–32` (roughly 2–3/day)
- **Hard minimum floor**: `14` (1/day)
- **Stretch**: `36` if energy and schedule hold.

### Domain Guardrails

- Job/Interview/Skills: 45–55%
- Content: 20–25%
- Personal Project: 15–20%
- Business/Corp: 5–10%

## Sprint Goals (3–6 max)

- [ ] Re-establish morning deep-work habit on at least 10 of 14 days.
- [ ] Complete two interview-prep loops (system design + behavioral).
- [ ] Publish three content pieces from planned outline backlog.
- [ ] Close one pending project milestone with documented handoff.
- [ ] Keep business/admin queue under 48-hour response latency.

## Sprint Backlog (high-level)

**Job/Interview/Skills**
- System design drills and mock review.
- Finish one architecture write-up for internal portfolio.

**Content**
- Convert saved hooks into three short-form drafts.
- Batch edit and schedule posts twice per week.

**Personal Project**
- Ship one feature slice and add smoke-test coverage.

**Business/Corp**
- Triage partnership replies and update tracker.
