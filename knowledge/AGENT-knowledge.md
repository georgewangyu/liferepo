# AGENT-knowledge

## Purpose

Define reusable workflows for capturing, refining, and organizing knowledge.

## Workflow

1. Capture research questions and hypotheses.
2. Collect sources and synthesize findings.
3. Distill into reusable models, patterns, and decision support.
4. Route actionable outputs to principles, projects, or daily planning.

## Research Lifecycle

- Allow rough exploratory notes early.
- Refine progressively into structured artifacts.
- Separate prompts, raw findings, and polished conclusions.
- Keep a clear path from exploration to actionable outputs.

## Quality Rules

- Keep raw notes separate from polished artifacts.
- Link claims to source evidence.
- Prefer structured summaries over loose note dumps.
- Use topic-oriented organization for long-term retrieval.
